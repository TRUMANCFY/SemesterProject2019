Coridel Entertainment is a South Korean entertainment company established in 2015 by Tyler Kwon.


== History ==
Coridel Entertainment was founded in 2015 by Tyler Kwon as a subsidiary of the Coridel Group, based in New York City, United States. It merged the same year with a record label called Clear Company, which managed the K-pop girl group Playback and Jeff Bernat's Korean concerts and releases.On February 28, 2016, Jessica Jung signed with Coridel Entertainment following her split from Girls' Generation in 2014 and departure from her former agency SM Entertainment in 2015. On May 17, 2016, she released her solo debut album under the label titled With Love, J, with the lead single "Fly". On December 10, 2016 she released her second solo album under the label titled Wonderland, with her lead single "Wonderland".In April 2017, Actor Ryu Tae-joon signed with Coridel Entertainment.
In September 2018, Singer Kevin Woo signed with Coridel Entertainment.
